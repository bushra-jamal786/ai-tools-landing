About AI Tools

AI tools are online apps that use artificial intelligence to help people complete tasks faster. For students and beginners, they can support homework, note-taking, presentations, and basic creative work.

You do not need advanced technical skills to use them. Most tools work through simple text boxes, templates, and easy-to-follow steps. When used responsibly, they can make learning and daily productivity easier.
